[✅] STOOL PHOTO VALIDATION ENGINE
  - Canvas-based pixel color analysis (HSL)
  - Downsampled to 150px max for speed
  - Color classification: brownish, greenish, white, black, vivid, blue
  - Rejection criteria:
    · Document/Paper: white pixels > 55%
    · Colorful image: vivid pixels > 35%
    · Blue image: blue pixels > 25%
    · No stool detected: stool-like pixels < 8%
    · Too dark: black pixels > 70%
  - Localized rejection modal (KO/EN/JA) with specific reason
  - Haptic feedback on rejection (notificationWarning)
